# Possible structure for Preliminary Project Statement
1. What's the problem - onboarding for app users 
2. Why is it a problem - many people learn languages, many people are moving, (https://mircealungu.com/projects/zeeguu) 
3. What's your solution 
	1. list of **problems** that users actually have and corresponding **design solutions**
4. Method: user-centered UX evaluation - iterative, process
	1. step 1: evaluate current UI with several users
	2. step 2: prioritize improvements
	3. step 3: implement and go to step 1
5. What's the implication of the solution - more people will study
